"Develops a uniform body, corrects postures, restores vitality physical, invigorates the mind and uplifts the spirit "- There is no better description of this modality that we love so much and that improves strength, balance, flexibility, body awareness and still soothes the soul. In our pilates, we work with a mix of pilates, aerial (with the columpyo) and soil to better serve the student. We seek to maintain a class that is interesting and helps the student in his goal, making him a pleasant moment of body care. Calms the soul and is the best meeting of life, which is yours.
